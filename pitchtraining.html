Pitch trainings

Recent studies and surveys have shown that having a skill alongside a degree puts you some level higher than other applicants during a job interview.

Survey has also shown that interviewers and human resource managers are no less interested in degree than they are in what you are bringing to the table. That's where we come in, never be stranded or dependent with our various training programs.

Our school of training offer the best learning conditions with the neccessary tools and human power. We dont just organise trainings and leave you, we render help and services that can assist in further development of that skill. We have flexible payment options and schedule to help balance your training and other prior involvements you may have.
